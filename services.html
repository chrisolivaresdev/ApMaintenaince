Services

ApMaintenances offers a full range of remodeling services, from initial design to completion.

cleaning

Our cleaning service for houses and buildings is the perfect solution to keep your space impeccable and in optimal conditions.

Electricity

Our home and building electricity service is the reliable and professional solution for all your electrical needs. We have a team of highly trained and certified experts, ready to provide you with safe, efficient and quality electricity services.

Plumbing

We understand the importance of having a well-maintained and working plumbing system in your home or building. That's why we're committed to offering comprehensive plumbing solutions, from initial installation through repairs and periodic maintenance.

Drywall

Our drywall installation service for houses and buildings is the perfect solution to create divisions, linings and quality interior finishes. We have a team of highly trained and experienced drywall installation experts, ready to provide you with an impeccable and satisfactory result.

Paint

Our painting service for houses and buildings is the perfect solution to transform and beautify your space. Our painting service covers a wide range of needs, both indoors and outdoors.

Doors

Our door service for houses and buildings is the ideal solution to improve the security, functionality and aesthetics of your space. We offer comprehensive door solutions, from initial installation through necessary repairs and adjustments.

Floors

Our flooring service encompasses a wide variety of options, including hardwood, laminate, vinyl, ceramic tile, natural stone, and more. We work with high quality materials and we have the necessary experience to offer you professional advice in choosing the most suitable type of flooring for your needs, preferences and budget.

General maintenance

At our company, we understand the importance of caring for and preserving your home or building, both in terms of functionality and aesthetics. That's why we are committed to providing a wide range of maintenance services ranging from electrical and plumbing work to painting, door and floor repairs, and general cleaning.
